Global Distribution of Billionaires 2024

This dashboard visualises the global distribution of billionaires' wealth in 2024, as well as the evolution of billionaires over time. Billionaires have a significant impact on the global economy, shaping industries, innovation, and even geopolitics. As the wealthiest individuals, their influence extends beyond their companies and ventures, reaching into philanthropic efforts and public policy. Understanding the distribution and growth of billionaires around the world offers valuable insights into economic disparities, regional economic powerhouses, and shifts in global wealth trends.

This visualisation provides an in-depth look at the world’s billionaires in 2024, offering a snapshot of their wealth distribution, the industries they dominate, and how their numbers have evolved over time. Explore the interactive visualisations to delve into the dynamics of global wealth and see how billionaires shape our world.

    Evolution of Billionaires

    Understanding how the number of billionaires and their wealth has evolved over time offers valuable context to the current distribution seen in 2024. This historical perspective sheds light on economic changes, technological advancements and policy shifts that have influenced wealth accumulation. By examining the trends from 1996 to 2024, you can see how global events, such as financial crises and market booms, have affected the growth in the number of billionaires and their combined net worth.

    The following line chart illustrates these changes, showing the total number of billionaires and their cumulative wealth over time. Use the slider at the bottom to adjust the time period displayed on the chart, focusing on specific years to observe detailed trends. By linking the trends in this chart with the wealth distribution visualisations seen in the previous sections, you can see how the shifts in billionaire growth over time have contributed to the economic landscape we see today.
